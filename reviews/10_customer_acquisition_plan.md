# Customer Acquisition Plan — LeWM-VC Design Partners

## Target Market: Surveillance Operators with Machine Analytics

Focus on organizations managing large-scale camera deployments with pain points in bandwidth, storage, edge processing, and machine analytics. LeWM-VC solves these problems: lower bitrates at better detection accuracy than H.265, aligned with MPEG VCM.

---

## Step 1: Identify the Right Targets

Prioritize organizations that:
- Run large camera fleets (thousands of cameras)
- Deal with remote/edge deployments, cellular bandwidth constraints, or high storage costs
- Already use or test AI analytics, edge VMS, or advanced codecs
- Are open to pilots/LOIs — have innovation budgets or face scaling issues

### Top Categories and Examples

| Category | Target Companies |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Large system operators/integrators** | Convergint, Johnson Controls, Securitas Technology, ADT Commercial, Allied Universal |
| **Camera/VMS manufacturers** | Hikvision, Dahua, Axis, Hanwha Vision, Motorola Solutions (Avigilon), Bosch, Genetec |
| **Remote video monitoring (RVM)** | Companies piloting AI (e.g., SARA) or using Digital Barriers EdgeVis |
| **Critical infrastructure & smart cities** | Utilities, transportation authorities, ports, correctional facilities |
| **Specialized surveillance ops** | Retail chains, warehouses, public safety networks |

## Step 2: Build a Pipeline of 5+ Prospects

### Warm Intros First (Highest Conversion)
- Leverage LinkedIn, existing network, advisors, conference contacts (ISC West, GSX, IFSEC, MPEG events)
- Post on LinkedIn: "Seeking surveillance operators for LeWM-VC pilot"

### Targeted Outreach
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator for titles: CTO, VP Technology/Innovation, Director of Video Operations, Head of AI/ML Surveillance, Chief Security Officer
- Hunter.io or Apollo for email discovery
- Attend/sponsor events on edge AI video compression or VCM surveillance

### Cold but Personalized
Reference their specific pain point. Offer a 30-minute call + demo on their sample data.

### Pipeline Targets
20–30 outreaches → 5–8 conversations → 2–3 LOI discussions → 1 signed by Day 100. Repeat for next 4.

---

## Step 3: The Sales & LOI Process

### Validate Fit
- Share LeWM-VC one-pager with specific benefits (62% storage savings, +7.2 pp accuracy)
- Offer to run on their deployment clips (anonymized) for quick proof

### Pilot Structure
- They provide sample deployment data + success metrics
- You deploy LeWM-VC (Docker/edge package) in their environment
- Mutual NDA + clear IP terms
- Path to paid contract if successful

### LOI Template
Non-binding intent to collaborate. Covers: timelines, data sharing, success criteria, next steps toward commercial agreement. Emphasize "co-development" value.

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **Risk** | Start with offline testing on their data. No production deployment until they're confident. |
| **Integration** | Docker package. Comparability to existing baselines (H.265). |
| **Standards alignment** | MPEG VCM submission in progress. LeWM-Eval methodology aligned with VCM CTC. |

## Step 4: Parallelize for 5 Customers

- **Hire/Support BD early** — VP BD with security industry experience is the first non-founder hire
- **Leverage partners** — Integrators (Convergint, etc.) can introduce multiple end operators
- **Marketing pull** — Publish benchmarks, attend VCM/MPEG workshops, position as "VCM-ready"
- **Pipeline tracking** — Simple CRM: Contacted → Meeting → Data share → Pilot → LOI
- **Timeline hack** — Run parallel lightweight evaluations while negotiating one LOI. One signed deal makes the next 4 easier (social proof).
